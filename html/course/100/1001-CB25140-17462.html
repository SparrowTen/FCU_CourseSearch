課程描述
本課程主要目標為提供學生一套跨文化視野，協助每一位學生統整在逢甲大學的基礎教育、通識教育、商管專業課程以及生活經驗，並有系統地紀錄與省思自己在留學期間的專業學習與跨文化學習與調適經驗。 學生紀錄與省思主要涵蓋四個層面 (1)專業知識與技能的學習心得，(2)英語能力的提升，若為國際生，則為在逢甲期間中文能力的提升，(3)跨文化經驗，以及(4)生活調適。這些層面的經驗將被放在跨文化的視野下來檢視，透過反思與撰寫一系列的作業問題，將有助於學生成為一個具有人文素養與國際溝通能力的跨國企業管理專才。
The objective of the course is to provide students with a set of cross-cultural framework, which enable students to integrate general education, professional courses, and other living experience in Feng Chia University. Then students will have to systematically record and reflect their professional learning and cross-cultural learning while studying in another country. Students will reflect and write down experiences in the following four areas: 1. Learning experience in accumulating professional knowledge and skills. 2. Improvement in English (or Chinese for international students at Feng Chia University). 3. Cross cultural experience. 4. Adaptation and adjustment in living in a foreign country. Through writing a series of reflecting assignments, students will acquire essential elements that are necessary for becoming an expert and a leader in the arena of international business.
